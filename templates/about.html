Our Mission

The Wellness Autism Screening Tool was developed with a clear mission: to make early autism identification accessible, straightforward, and supportive. We believe that early identification leads to earlier intervention, which research has consistently shown to improve outcomes for individuals on the autism spectrum. Our goal is to provide a screening tool that helps individuals and families take that important first step.

The Technology Behind Our Tool

Our assessment tool utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze responses to evidence-based screening questions. We've implemented a sophisticated ensemble model that combines three powerful classifiers:

XGBoost
A gradient boosting framework known for its performance and speed
LightGBM
A gradient boosting framework that uses tree-based learning algorithms
CatBoost
A machine learning algorithm that can handle categorical features automatically

By combining these models using a soft voting mechanism, we achieve a more reliable and robust prediction than any single model could provide alone.

Privacy and Ethics

We take privacy and ethical considerations seriously. User data is stored securely, and assessment results are only visible to the user.

We're transparent about the limitations of our tool - while it provides a helpful screening, it is not a diagnostic instrument. We encourage users to consult with healthcare professionals for formal evaluation and diagnosis.
